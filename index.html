Our Academic Offerings

Kinder 1 and 2

These are early childhood education stages, designed to prepare children for formal schooling, focusing on foundational skills and social development.

Grade 1 to 6

This comprises the elementary level, where students learn core subjects like Math, Science, and Language, building essential knowledge and skills.

Grade 7 to 10

This is the junior high school level, where students delve deeper into academic subjects, exploring various disciplines and preparing for senior high school or technical-vocational tracks.

Senior High School Strands We Offer

Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM)

This strand provides preparation for students in using science, math, engineering, and technology to solve real-life problems and advance national development.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

This strand focuses on the study of human behavior and societal changes, and analysis of arts, culture, literature, and politics. It involves political science, anthropology, linguistics, psychology, and communication.

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

This strand focuses on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for.

Our Facilities

School building that ensures students quality education.

Canteen

School canteens provide students with nutritious meals, promoting healthy eating habits and supporting their academic performance. They also serve as a social hub and offer a convenient food option for students and staff.

Library

Libraries are essential spaces that provide access to a wealth of information and resources, fostering learning, research, and personal growth. They offer diverse collections of books, periodicals, digital media, and other materials, catering to a wide range of interests and needs.

Gymnasium

A gymnasium, often shortened to "gym," is a large outdoor space designed for athletic activities and school events. It typically includes equipment for sports like basketball, volleyball, and badminton. Gymnasiums are used for events, physical education classes, sports practices, competitions, and recreational activities.

Audio Visual Room

An Audio Visual Room (AVR) is a dedicated space equipped with audio and visual technology, such as projectors, screens, speakers, and sometimes recording equipment. It's designed to facilitate presentations, lectures, film screenings, and other activities that benefit from multimedia resources, enhancing communication and learning.

Science Laboratory

A wet science laboratory is a space designed for experiments and research that involve liquScience laboratories, encompassing both wet and dry labs, are essential learning spaces where students engage in hands-on experimentation and exploration. They foster a deeper understanding of scientific concepts, develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and ignite a passion for scientific inquiry.

Computer Laboratory

A computer laboratory is a dedicated space equipped with computers, networking infrastructure, and software applications, providing students with access to technology for learning, research, and project work. It's a place where they can develop digital literacy skills, explore programming, and utilize technology as a tool for various academic pursuits..
